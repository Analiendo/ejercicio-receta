Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic dish combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meat.

    Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter, or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, cooked meat, herbs
